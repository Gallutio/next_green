What it does

Daily logging

Chat about your day — water, food, transport, power. The AI fills in the numbers.

Dashboard

See your footprint in kilograms of CO₂ next to your water and energy use.

AI coaching

Short, specific tips based on what you actually logged. No generic advice.

Global context
