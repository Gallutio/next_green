The 17 Sustainable Development Goals

The UN's seventeen goals — the framework NextGreen ties back to. Our work focuses most directly on goals 12, 13 and 15, but they're all connected.

SDG 1. No Poverty

The goal is to end all forms of poverty and help ensure that no one is left behind.

SDG 2. Zero Hunger

The goal is to eliminate hunger and promote food security and sustainable agriculture.

SDG 3. Good Health

The goal is to ensure a healthy life and well-being for all age groups.

SDG 4. Quality Education

The goal is to provide fair and high-quality educational opportunities to everyone.

SDG 5. Gender Equality

The goal is to achieve gender equality and strengthen the rights and interests of women and girls.

SDG 6. Clean Water

The goal is to guarantee clean water and sanitation facilities for everyone.

SDG 7. Clean Energy

The goal is to expand access to affordable, stable, and sustainable energy.

SDG 8. Decent Work

It is a goal to promote quality jobs and sustainable economic growth.

SDG 9. Industry and Innovation

It is a goal to support sustainable development by strengthening industry, innovation, and infrastructure.

SDG 10. Reduced Inequalities

The goal is to reduce inequality both within and outside the country.

SDG 11. Sustainable Cities

The goal is to create safe, resilient, and sustainable cities and communities.

SDG 12. Responsible Consumption

The goal is to spread responsible consumption and production methods.

SDG 13. Climate Action

It is a goal to promote urgent action to address climate change and its impacts.

SDG 14. Life Below Water

It is a goal to conserve the ocean and marine resources and to use them sustainably.

SDG 15. Life on Land

The goal is to protect terrestrial ecosystems and safeguard forests and biodiversity.

SDG 16. Peace and Justice

The goal is to build peace, justice, and strong institutions.

SDG 17. Partnerships

The goal is to strengthen global partnerships to achieve goals.
