What is NextGreen?

NextGreen is a browser-based platform that empowers users to shrink their environmental footprint. You can log your daily habits — from paper use and food waste to energy consumption — and instantly see how your choices compare to sustainable benchmarks.

Our AI coach then reviews your habits and gives you simple, personalized tips to help you make greener choices every day.
