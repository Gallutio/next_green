What is NextGreen?

NextGreen is a browser-based platform empowering users to shrink their environmental footprint. You can log your daily habits like paper use, food waste, and energy consumption to instantly see how your choices compare to sustainable benchmarks.

Our AI coach reviews your habits and gives you simple, personalized tips to help you make greener choices every day.
