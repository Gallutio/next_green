What is NextGreen?

NextGreen is a browser-based platform where you log your daily habits — paper use, food waste, energy, transport — and get a reading on how your choices compare to sustainable benchmarks.

An AI coach then reviews what you logged and suggests a couple of simple, specific tweaks to try the next day. Nothing dramatic; just the stuff that adds up.
